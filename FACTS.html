FACTS ABOUT GENDER BASED VILENCE

Globally, 1 in 3 women have been beaten,
coerced into sex,or abused in some other way,
usually by someone they know.
Around 641 million women worldwide
have experienced at least one coincidence
of physical and sexual violence from
a romantic partner.
As many as 38% of murders of women
globally are committed by their intimate partners.
In some countries, the world Bank
estimates that violence against women
can cost up to 3.7% of the countries
GDP in lost productivity
thus impacting the capacities
of many families to earn.
